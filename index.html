About Me

Where Code Meets Community

I'm a full-stack developer and staff manager who got into this by running Minecraft servers as a kid and never really stopped. What started as configuring plugins turned into writing them, building networks, and eventually managing entire staff teams.

10+ years in and I still get the same kick from watching a server hit capacity with stable TPS. I've worked on some of the biggest names in MC, hired and trained staff teams, and built the tools to keep everything running smoothly.

I also build web apps, dashboards, and internal tools. But the community side is just as important to me. A good server needs good people running it, and I know how to build both.
